## What it is

A 6,000-word essay on the history of the prefatory paragraph in
typography. Published as a long-form scrollable web piece with custom
typesetting per section.

## My role

Wrote the words. Designed the page. Wrote the JavaScript that handled the
scroll-triggered transitions between typefaces.

## Why I&apos;m proud

It won a quiet typography award. More importantly, three people I admire
told me they read it twice.
