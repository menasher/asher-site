## The brief

A Series-B fintech&apos;s consumer iOS app needed a clean v3 — v2 had been
shipped under pressure and accumulated three years of debt. The other half
of the brief: extract a design system the web team could share.

## My role

Led design across v3. Owned the system extraction. Worked with engineering
on the SwiftUI implementation.

## Outcomes

- v3 ships with **47% smaller binary** (fewer image assets, vector
  everything)
- Design system in use across 3 product surfaces
- Halved the time-to-prototype for new features
