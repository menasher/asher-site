## The brief

A public cultural institution&apos;s internal reporting tool — used by ~30
researchers and 4 program leads — had grown organically over a decade. The
dashboards were slow, the export pipeline was slower, and onboarding a new
researcher took two weeks of shadowing.

## My role

Owned the redesign end-to-end: research interviews, IA, visual design,
frontend implementation. The backend team owned the query layer.

## Outcomes

- Report generation **40 min → 90 sec** (warm cache)
- Onboarding **2 weeks → 2 days**
- The shape of the tool finally matches the shape of the work
